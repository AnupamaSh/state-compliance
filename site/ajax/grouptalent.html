About GroupTalent
Founded in 2011, GroupTalent is a hiring platform that serves as an employment marketplace for programmers and employers looking for technical expertise. At times referred to as a “dating website for developers,” employers can hire programmers on a trial basis, and potential employees can test the waters with a new employer before making a serious commitment.
