About GovTogetherBC
GovTogetherBC provides citizens with a central location to find government consultation and community volunteering opportunities and get directly involved. Beyond simplicity and ease of use, the site promotes greater government transparency by publishing the full results of completed public consultation projects.

Challenges
Since consultation opportunities link to off-site information and sign-up pages on agency or department websites, there is little standardization of available projects. Moreover, since the site acts as an aggregated list, rather than a platform for consultation, there is little opportunity for citizen collaboration or community building.
