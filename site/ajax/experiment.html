About Experiment
The project was launched in 2012 under the name Microryza by two University of Washington graduates, Denny Luan and Cindy Yu, seeking to understand how the Internet could be used to help fund scientific projects that are left behind by the traditional grant funding model. The site relaunched as Experiment.com in February 2014.
Rather than provide funders with perks, a traditional part of crowdfunding, funders of projects crowdfunded through Experiment get access to discussions around the experimental process.
