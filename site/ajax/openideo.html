OpenIDEO
About OpenIDEO
OpenIDEO is an online platform developed by the design and innovation firm, IDEO, to cultivate creative solutions to pressing social problems.
OpenIDEO hosts specific challenges by joining with a partner organization to pose a “big question” to the greater OpenIDEO community. The community then works together to research potential solutions to the problem, collaborate on ideas, and evaluate the solution to the problem that has been developed through this community process.

Historical Information
Launched in August 2010, OpenIDEO is an open innovation platform that allows IDEO to gain a broader perspective and reach by relying on community participation.
