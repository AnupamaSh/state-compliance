Apply for Authorization

Thank you for using NYU's Academic Accreditation Compliance Instrument. Based on your responses, your organization should apply for authorization in New Mexico. Please see below for information and instructions about how to apply:

  • Apply to The New Mexico Higher Education Department for program and/or institutional authorization;
  • You have indicated that your organization is a multi-institution system . In New Mexico, each institution within your organization must apply for authorization individually;
  • Applications for authorization to New Mexico Higher Education Department must include either proof of current accreditation status (by a body recognized by BOTH the U.S. Department of Education and the New Mexico Higher Education Department) OR include a plan to become accredited within three years;
  • The initial authorization application process generally takes 6 to 9 months.
