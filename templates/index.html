About the NYU State Authorization Advisor

NYU’s State Authorization Advisor is an online tool that provides institutions with the opportunity to concretely learn what actions--and costs are required to achieve and maintain compliance with state regulations.. The tool’s series of diagnostic questions tie directly to state agency policies and fees. Institutional characteristics and activities are collected through a progressive wizard, resulting in a concrete, customized action plan for next steps. This user-friendly interface streamlines, the task of navigating complex regulations, especially for individuals who are not attorneys or policy experts.
